Type: Primary

Name: Roger Eavedrop

Background
  • Date of Birth: 10/23/1990
  • Gender: Male
  • Location: Chicago, Illinois
  • Work place: Freelancing , Software Engineer
  • Technology Level: Medium
Main Points

Roger is a very hardworking and sociable person; he fits in almost every place and he likes to learn about new cultures around himself.

  • Experienced with certain mobile apps;
  • Dislikes about language barriers;
  • He doesn't like being interrupted or not having the chance to speak with someone;
  • He likes to talk with others; he considers a day lost if he was all alone
Goals
  • Practical Goals: to travel and to interact with as many cultures as possible
  • Personal Goals: to work with different people
  • Business Goals: to be a very successful freelancer
Frustrations and Pain Points
  • He doesn't like the apps that don't feature dark themes
  • If the app doesn't involve himself very much, he quickly loses his interest
Detailed Description

Steve Eavedrop is a proffesional freelancer; working remotely with his own team was his dream job. Besides, he likes a lot to interact with different people and cultures.

He once interacted with a person unable to hear and he felt horrible because he couldn't understand what he was trying to say using ASL.

From that moment on, he became eager to learn how to interact with as much different people as possible.

  • Practical Goals: being efficient, talking straight to the point
  • Personal Goals: not feeling stupid, getting an adequate amount of work done, learning a little bit of every language;
  • Business Goals like: networking with a lot of people
  • High experience and expertise regarding mobile & iOS app development and usage;
  • Uses this kind of apps in the everyday routine
References

  • The Persona Lifecycle, Keeping People in Mind Throughout Product Design, John Pruitt & Tamara Adlin;

Scenarios

Roger Eavedrop would use this app in order to collaborate with people unable to speak from his working branch in order to exchange ideas and to interact with certain clients.

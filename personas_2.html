Type: Customer

Name: Jennifer Roberts

Background
  • Date of Birth: 01/11/1985
  • Gender: Female
  • Location: New York, New York
  • Job: Psychologist
  • Technology Level: Medium
Main Points

Jennifer Roberts is a psychologist at Saint-Marie College for children with dissabilities; she likes a lot helping others and talking especially with children.

  • Experienced in using mobile apps;
  • Dislikes when she can't understand her pacients;
  • She doesn't like using hand dictionaries
  • She likes to talk with children
Goals
  • Practical Goals: to become a successful psychologist
  • Personal Goals: to help as much people as possible
Frustrations and Pain Points
  • She doesn't like dark themed designs for apps.
  • She is scarred of apps not featuring a help menu.
Detailed Description

Jennifer Roberts is a psychologist; ever since she was little, she knew that talking with others can help solving every issue she has.

Once she saw a man at the marketplace who was doing hand poses in order to express what he intended to buy, but the shop assistant couldn't understand his order.

From that moment on, she became determined in learning as much as possible about different means of communications.

  • Practical Goals: being efficient, talking straight to the point
  • Personal Goals: not feeling stupid, breaking language barriers;
  • She doesn't use her phone very often
  • She likes respecting the rules given by the apps; interacts easily following the rules
References

  • An introduction to personas and how to create them;
  • The Persona Lifecycle, Keeping People in Mind Throughout Product Design, John Pruitt & Tamara Adlin;

Scenarios

Jennifer Roberts would use this app in order to talk with certain pacients, learning a new language in a fun and easy way and is eager to help other people learning the ASL.

Type: Negative

Name: Karen Jensen

Background
  • Date of Birth: 11/09/1938
  • Gender: Female
  • Location: Washington, DC
  • Teacher Wyoming Catholic College
  • Technology Level: Low
Main Points

Karen Jensen was a teacher at a Catholic College; she never liked the technology and she chose to live a much more simpler life than others, away from the robotic "carnage" featured by the world.

  • Doesn't like using smart phones
  • Prefers the phone instead of a computer
  • Talkative and wise
  • Knows and speaks a lot of languages
Goals
  • Practical Goals: to learn the ASL
  • Personal Goals: to become less scared of technology
Frustrations and Pain Points
  • She hates all the things related to technology
  • Dislikes reading long instructions
  • Technical words may influence her behaviour
  • Wearing glasses, she may harm her eyes if she watches a display for a prolonged time
Detailed Description

Karen Jensen lived all her life helping orphaned children by working as a teacher at a Catholic College.

One day she called the parents of a child who wasn't speaking to her relating bad behaviour.

When the parents arrived to school, she was informed that their children was unable to hear; she felt so horrible that she cried for the entire day.

  • Practical Goals: speaking with every person regardless of language barriers
  • Personal Goals like: not feeling stupid, helping other people;
  • Not experienced with mobile apps
  • Not using the phone very often
